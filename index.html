The 11-Section Template

1

What Launched — Company, product, access, pricing signal.

2

Version Context — Previous version delta, velocity, what’s still broken.

3

What’s Actually New — Genuine novelty vs. recycled packaging.

4

Technical Snapshot — Architecture, training data, scale signals.

5

Limitations & Flags — Dealbreakers, monitoring items, acceptable trade-offs.

6

Relevance Matrix — Three-ring analysis: direct teams → research labs → platforms.

7

Competitive Positioning — Current standing, moat, who should worry.

8

The “So What” — What changes, what’s the risk, one action item.

9

Scorecard — Novelty + Execution + Threat + Signal = /20.

10

Reception Check — Social pulse, press narrative, reality gap.

11

Tags — Category labels for cross-referencing.

Each teardown ends with a 10-question quiz covering all sections. Score 8/10 or higher to pass.
